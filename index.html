Pixel's war

In it's current state, Pixel's War is a browser based game prototype inspired by the Famicom Wars / Advance Wars. This project is written using basic web technologies: HTML / CSS and JS. The prototype allow you to play versus another player on the same computer. Be the best commander, lead your army to the victory and show to your defeated friend who is the smartest!

Give it a try!

  • Squared 64 tiles (8*8)

Controls

  • Left Click: select a unit or attack a in-range enemy unit while a unit is selected
  • Arrows or ZQSD: move unit while selected.
  • Enter: valid a unit move while selected
  • Escape: cancel a unit move while selected
  • Space: capture a building while a infantry is selected and on a cell containing a building

Units

Infantery

They are weak but they are cheap, so buy some meatboys and send them to the butcher. Infantry can capture building, so it's a vital unit for your economy!

Jeep

Not really powerfull, but they cover long distance and they are relatively cheap. Ideal for harassing infantry and occupying strategic positions on the map.

Tank

Costly unit with a lot of fire power and a high defense bonus, making them hard to destroy! Nice, if you can afford them.

Artillery

With a large attack range and a strong firepower, they are a very usefull unit! Unfortunately, they are costly and they do not have good mobility capacity.

Landscapes

Grass

A good old grass. Don't give any cover and more costly than road. But, hey, sometimes, you doesn't have the choice!

Moutain

Moutains are very costly to move on but they give you a very high defense bonus.

Forest

What's better than a little forest ballad? Costly to move on, but give a little defense bonus.

Road

The most convenient way to reach a place. The less costly landscape to move on, no defense bonus.

Water

Only ships and flying units can move on. Be carefull, it's wet.

Buildings

City

People lives in and works in these places. This is where the money is printed. Without cities, you cannot afford to buy some fancy military units to show your power to the world.

Factory

It is where new units are created and trained. It smells terrible. They currently recruit, send you CV.

Hospital

No one like to be there but it's better than dying in the wild. This is where your unit can get healing and recover from damage they received.
